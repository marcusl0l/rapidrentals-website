About Us

Rapid Rentals is a family owned business that aims to provide a high quality, friendly rental service. We specialise in light truck and Cargo Van hire which is generally used for moving furniture and people with ease.

We are situated in Takanini, not far from the Takanini off ramp and just across the road from Burger King and the new Zed service station. Come and see us now!
