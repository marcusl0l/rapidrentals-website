About Rapid Rentals Takanini

Welcome to Rapid Rentals, your trusted family-owned truck, trailer and van hire service in South Auckland. Since opening our doors in Takanini, we've been helping locals and businesses with affordable, reliable vehicle rentals for moving furniture, business deliveries, and home projects.
